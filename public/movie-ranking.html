The Best Movies According To Randy

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Godfather

This is a very well-made gangster movie that was set in the late 40s and teaches about putting family first and abiding by one's own principles


Django Unchained

This is a modern film set in the Wild West and tells a story of Django, a runaway slave from Southern USA that was bought and freed by a dentist-turned-bounty-hunter, Dr. King Schultz, who later on helped Django free his wife, Broomhilda.


Star Wars

The coolest of all science fiction movies that tells about the galaxy's rebellion against an oppresive empire.
